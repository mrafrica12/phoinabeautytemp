The Phoina
Story.

Phoina Beauty was founded in 2018 with a simple but powerful belief: that African women deserve world-class beauty experiences in their own city. What began as a small studio in Nairobi has grown into a full beauty ecosystem — a premium salon, a professional academy, and a curated product line.

Our founder, a trained makeup artist with over a decade of experience, saw a gap in the market. The best-in-class beauty education and luxury studio experiences were either out of reach or didn't celebrate the full spectrum of African beauty. Phoina was built to change that.

Today, we serve thousands of clients, have trained over 500 graduates, and stock a hand-picked collection of beauty products that work beautifully on every complexion. But our mission remains the same: to make every woman who walks through our doors feel seen, celebrated, and stunning.
